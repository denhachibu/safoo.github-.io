About us

At SAFOO DHOW TOURS AND SAFARIS, we unlock the magic of Kenya's coastline. Embark on a captivating journey aboard our traditional dhow boats, where turquoise waters and vibrant coral reefs await. Encounter playful dolphins in their natural habitat and savor the bounty of the sea with fresh seafood experiences and a journey to be a perfect blend of relaxation, discovery, and exhilarating encounters with marine life. Our passionate team of local experts curates unforgettable adventures, ensuring every moment is filled with wonder and discovery. Let us redefine your expectations of coastal exploration – contact us today and craft your dream marine escape
